symbiosis

The Symbiosis Colleges present in India provide students with courses in a variety of disciplines like Management, Engineering, Law, and Design.
Symbiosis Colleges are the academic institutions of the Symbiosis Society which were established in the year 1971. The main aim behind the establishment
of Symbiosis College is that it would help foreign students who have come to India for studying. Today, the institute is meeting its goal as students from
more than 85 countries are studying in the Symbiosis Colleges.
